Who am I?

I am Corey Crooks; a front-end programmer.

     I started my enthusiasm back in middle school when I got a hold of HTML. The idea of writing at a keyboard, and making fantastic images appear on screen was simply fascinating. I started with an early local site with no hosting whatsoever written purely in HTML.

     My intrigue has grown since then along with the innovations of the language. I have bolstered my own skillsets through both self–taught work, and peer–to–peer work through volunteer and academic studies. I am thankful for the skills I have developed with my Bachelor's degree in software programming, and enthusiastic towards the further knowledge I will gather in my professional work going forward.

     Today, I use my free time engaging in new technological systems to further expand my knowledge. The ever–changing climate of website design draws me in, but I am no stranger to back–end work in C#, Python, or even SQL! This site serves as both a benchmark for my current front-end progress, and also a portfolio for what I've done in the past! I have pages for my work experience, photography hobby, and design philosophies.
